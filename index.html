    What we Offer


    • 01 GENERAL TERM LOAN

      The Scheme is intended to provide financial assistance to new units / expansion / modernization / diversification.

    • 02 WORKING CAPITAL

      To extend financial assistance to Manufacturing & Processing sector including Rice Mill units to meet their working capital/additional working capital requirements.

    • 03 EQUIPMENT FINANCE

      Assistance under the scheme will be for purchase and erection of identifiable items of plant and machinery including generators for modernization/expansion/balancing/replacement.

    • 04 BILL FINANCE

      For discounting the Bills of TNEB as per purchase order/ Turnkey projects.

    • 05 CENTRAL SUBSIDY

      Technology upgradation

    • 06 STATE SUBSIDY

      Investment in Plant & Machinery

    • 07 MEGA SUBSIDY

      Granting Subsidy /Incentives for the investment made towards land, building, plant and Machinery, environment protection infrastructure equipments and Soft Loan /Investment promotion subsidy for net output VAT + CST paid to the Government for the Medium / Large / Mega projects set up in Tamil Nadu.

    • 08 GENERATOR SUBSIDY

      Purchase of new Generator upto the capacity of 320 KVA (Inclduing the existing Generator Capacity)
